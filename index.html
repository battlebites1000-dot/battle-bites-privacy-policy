Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 19, 2025

Battle Bites is a casual mobile game that shows ads using Google AdMob. We take your privacy seriously and explain below what data may be collected, how it’s used, and the choices you have.

Summary

  • We do not collect information that directly identifies you (like your name or email) inside the app.
  • The app uses third-party services (notably Google / AdMob) that may collect device information and an advertising identifier to show and measure ads.
  • Gameplay choices (e.g., which animal you pick) may be stored in our backend in an anonymized way to compute aggregate statistics.

Information We Collect

Depending on your device and settings, the app and its SDK partners may process:

  • App activity: Basic gameplay interactions (e.g., which option you selected, counters for wins/losses) used to show aggregate stats and improve the game.
  • Device information & identifiers: Such as device model, OS version, IP address (for general location like country/region), crash logs, and Advertising ID (AAID) on Android, collected by AdMob/Google Play services for ad delivery, frequency capping, and fraud prevention.
